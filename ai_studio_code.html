New Tax Regime Slabs for FY 2025-26

The new income tax slabs under the new tax regime for FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27) are: Nil for income up to Rs. 4 lakh, 5% for Rs. 4 lakh - Rs. 8 lakh, 10% for Rs. 8 lakh - Rs. 12 lakh, 15% for Rs. 12 lakh - Rs. 16 lakh, 20% for Rs. 16 lakh - Rs. 20 lakh, 25% for Rs. 20 lakh - Rs. 24 lakh, and 30% for income above Rs. 24 lakh.

Income tax slabs define the tax rates applicable based on your annual income and differ under the old and new tax regimes. For FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27) and FY 2024-25 (AY 2025-26), the new regime is the default with revised slab rates and a higher rebate limit, while the old regime allows deductions like HRA and 80C.

Understanding the latest income tax slab rates helps salaried individuals, professionals, and senior citizens choose the most tax-efficient regime. Compare both regimes to determine which one will result in lower tax liability for your specific financial situation.
